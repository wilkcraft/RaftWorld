# 🌊 Raft World

**Raft World** is a NeoForge mod for Minecraft 1.21.1 that transforms the game into an ocean survival experience inspired by _Raft_.

After using `/startraft`, players are transported to a vast ocean world where floating debris becomes the primary source of resources, creating a unique survival loop centered around collection, expansion, and adaptation.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🌊 Endless Ocean Dimension

- Adds a custom dimension (`raftworld:raft`) made entirely of ocean.
- Players begin on a small wooden raft with minimal resources.
- No natural terrain or traditional resource generation.

---

### 📦 Floating Loot System

- Resources drift across the ocean surface, carried by simulated currents.
- Debris continuously appears around active players to keep exploration and collection engaging.
- Current loot includes:

  - Dirt
  - Oak Planks
  - Sticks
  - Rotten Flesh

---

### 🌬️ Ocean Currents

- Floating items drift from **east to west** across the ocean surface.
- Encourages players to position, build, and collect resources efficiently.
- Creates a gameplay loop inspired by drifting ocean debris.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

To begin a new Raft World adventure, use:

```text
/startraft
```

This command creates your starting raft, sets your respawn point, and teleports you to the custom ocean dimension.

---

## 🧠 Gameplay

The survival loop is simple but rewarding:

1. 🪝 Collect drifting resources.
2. 🧱 Expand your raft.
3. ⚒️ Craft tools and structures.
4. 🌊 Survive and continue growing.

---

## 🎮 Multiplayer

- Works in both singleplayer and multiplayer.
- Loot generation is handled server-side for consistent gameplay.
- Resource spawning scales with active players.

---

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- Custom ocean dimension.
- Tick-based server-side spawning.
- Water-only validation for floating debris.
- Simulated current movement.
- Multiplayer-compatible.

---

## 🛑 Compatibility
